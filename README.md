<h2>
<img src="https://img.icons8.com/color/28/error.png"/>
Problem Understanding
</h2>

<p>
Gig workers such as food delivery partners depend completely on daily earnings. Environmental disruptions like 
heavy rain, heatwaves, air pollution, traffic congestion, or sudden restrictions can significantly reduce their 
working hours, leading to <b>20–30% income loss</b>.
</p>

<p>
Existing insurance solutions mainly cover health, accidents, or vehicles, but there is no insurance model that 
protects gig workers from income loss caused by environmental disruptions.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>
<img src="https://img.icons8.com/color/28/user.png"/>
Persona-Based Scenario
</h2>

<h3>Ravi – Food Delivery Partner</h3>

<ul>
<li><b>Age:</b> 26</li>
<li><b>Platform:</b> Food delivery platforms</li>
<li><b>Daily Earnings:</b> ₹800–₹1000</li>
<li><b>Location:</b> Urban delivery zones</li>
</ul>

<h3>Scenario</h3>

<p>
Ravi begins his shift expecting to complete around 15–18 deliveries. Suddenly heavy rain begins in his area.
Orders reduce and roads become unsafe, forcing him to stop early. By the end of the week, he loses nearly 
<b>₹3000–₹4000</b> in income.
</p>

<p>
GigShield ensures Ravi receives <b>automatic compensation</b> whenever environmental disruptions affect his ability to work.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>
<img src="https://img.icons8.com/color/28/workflow.png"/>
Application Workflow
</h2>

<ol>
<li>Worker registers on the GigShield platform</li>
<li>System generates a Universal Worker Insurance ID</li>
<li>AI models analyze historical weather, pollution, and traffic data</li>
<li>System calculates weekly premium based on disruption risk</li>
<li>Platform monitors real-time environmental data using APIs</li>
<li>Parametric triggers activate when thresholds are crossed</li>
<li>Worker location and activity verified using GPS</li>
<li>Automatic payout transferred to worker bank account or wallet</li>
</ol>

<hr>

<h2>
<img src="https://img.icons8.com/color/28/money.png"/>
Weekly Premium Model
</h2>

<p>
GigShield uses a <b>dynamic weekly premium model</b> aligned with gig workers’ earning cycles.
</p>

<p>Premium calculation is based on:</p>

<ul>
<li>Environmental risk level in the worker’s delivery zone</li>
<li>Historical disruption frequency</li>
<li>Worker activity patterns</li>
<li>AI-based disruption probability prediction</li>
</ul>

<p>
Workers pay small weekly premiums, making the insurance <b>affordable and flexible</b>.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>
<img src="https://img.icons8.com/color/28/lightning-bolt.png"/>
Parametric Triggers
</h2>

<p>
Parametric insurance triggers are predefined conditions where payouts activate automatically.
</p>

<ul>
<li>Rainfall exceeding defined threshold</li>
<li>Air Quality Index reaching hazardous levels</li>
<li>Extreme temperature conditions</li>
<li>Severe traffic congestion</li>
<li>Government-imposed movement restrictions</li>
</ul>

<p>
Once detected, the system automatically triggers compensation without manual claim filing.
</p>

<h2>
<img src="https://img.icons8.com/color/28/artificial-intelligence.png"/>
AI / ML Integration
</h2>

<h3>Premium Calculation</h3>

<p>
Random Forest and Gradient Boosting models predict disruption risk and determine fair premiums.
</p>

<h3>Disruption Forecasting</h3>

<p>
LSTM time-series models analyze environmental data to forecast high-risk conditions.
</p>

<h3>Fraud Detection</h3>

<p>
Isolation Forest identifies suspicious activity such as abnormal GPS locations or claim patterns.
</p>

<h3>Risk Zone Mapping</h3>

<p>
AI generates heatmaps highlighting high-risk delivery zones.
</p>
